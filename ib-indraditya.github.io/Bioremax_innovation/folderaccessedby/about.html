Bioremax Innovation Pvt Ltd (BIPL)

About Bioremax Innovation

  • Bioremax Innovation Private Limited (BIPL) is a research startup company registered under I-Startup Rajasthan and MSE with the Rajasthan Government. Founded on 18th July 2024, BIPL specializes in developing innovative, environmentally sustainable solutions. Based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, the company is driven by a team of over six scientists and employees.
  • BIPL has developed an enzyme-based bioremediation process capable of breaking down oily sludge, crude sludge, and drill cuttings. This process is 100% natural, eco-friendly, and cost-effective. It transforms harmful hydrocarbons into non-toxic fatty acids using enzymes sourced from naturally occurring microbes.
  • BIPL is committed to advancing biotechnology for environmental sustainability with scalable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective solutions.
  • The company focuses on turning pollutants into non-toxic byproducts, helping industries meet environmental regulations effectively.
  • With a team of experts, BIPL continues to lead innovation for a greener and cleaner future.
  • As the first startup recognized by the Rajasthan government for science-based pollutant site restoration, BIPL leads in biotechnological innovation. Its proprietary super enzyme offers a pioneering solution for the safe treatment of oil-based environmental pollutants.
